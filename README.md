# Quizly

## Notes
- I used Google Stitch for designing.
- Questions aren't always serious; many of them are random and funny.

## Demo
- [Demo Link](https://abdalrhmangit.github.io/Quizly/)

## Info
- It is a simple quiz app with a countdown timer.
- You can choose an answer or skip the question without answering.
- The progress bar and circle help you see how much questions is remaining.
- The timer turns red if the remaining time is 30 seconds or less.
- Results are shown after skipping or completing the questions or when the timer runs out.
- The footer adjusts dynamically according to your screen size.
